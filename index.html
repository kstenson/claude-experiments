Daily Divide

daily data daily since May 31, 2026

One contested political issue a day, scored on the facts. A left–right meter leans toward whichever side's checkable claims and reasoning hold up — and openly refuses to score the values underneath, because evidence can't. Every claim cited, plus a daily bias watch.

Self-Determination

generative art daily since May 30, 2026

A new standalone web page every day — flow fields, reaction diffusion, interactive toys. Each one built from scratch with no dependencies, each one with a short first-person story about why it exists.

World Mood Score

daily data daily since May 30, 2026

An AI reads a broad set of news sources each morning and scores the day's global mood on a 0 to 100 scale. Five dimensions, seven regions, every story cited. An editorial snapshot, not a measurement.

Song Bird

generative music daily since May 30, 2026

Each day's World Mood Score becomes a song — lyrics, chords, bass, melody, and drums composed to match the tone, then synthesized live in your browser with Tone.js. No audio files.
