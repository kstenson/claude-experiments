How this works & how it's scored

Daily method

  • Identify the most-discussed claim in UK politics that day (PMQs, major speeches, broadsheet coverage, social reach).
  • Fact-check it against primary sources first (ONS, NAO, House of Commons Library, Hansard) and established fact-checkers (Full Fact, BBC Reality Check, Channel 4 FactCheck).
  • Score it 0–100 on the truth meter and write a plain-English verdict with the evidence and sources.
  • Append it to data/entries.json; the league recomputes automatically from every entry.

Truth meter bands

League scoring

  • Each figure's average score is the mean truth score of every claim attributed to them.
  • Higher = more truthful. The table also shows their best and worst single claim.
  • Claims are scored as used; where accuracy depends on the time period, the verdict says so.
